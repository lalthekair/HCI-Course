Latest Cafeteria News

Newest Dishes

An exciting new development in the cafeteria that students are going to love is the fcat that a bunch of new menu items have been added to the cafeteria menu.
In an effort to provide students with more healthy and sustainable food options, the cafeteria is adding new dishes to the menus. These new dishes are packed with nutrients and flavor, and they're sure to please even the pickiest eaters.

  • Lentil soup:
    This hearty and flavorful soup is a great source of protein and fiber. It's also vegan and gluten-free, making it a great option for students with dietary restrictions.
  • Chicken stir-fry:
    This dish is made with fresh chicken, vegetables, and sauce. It's a great source of protein and vitamins, and it's sure to satisfy even the hungriest students.
  • Black bean burgers:
    These burgers are a delicious and healthy alternative to beef burgers. They're packed with protein and fiber, and they can be topped with a variety of healthy toppings, like lettuce, tomato, and avocado.
  • Fruit smoothies:
    Smoothies are a great way to get a quick and healthy snack. They can be made with a variety of fruits, vegetables, and yogurt. They're a great source of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, and they're sure to energize students for the rest of the day.

These are just a few of the newest dishes that you can find in the cafeteria.


Window Seats

That's right, students can now enjoy their lunch with a view. The new benches are located in the front of the cafeteria, and they offer stunning views of the first gate.
Students are loving the new benches. They say that it's great to be able to eat their lunch while enjoying the greenery and sunshine. They also say that the new benches are a great place to socialize with their friends and classmates.
They are also an incredible chance for students to work while they eat, adding a sturdy table and comfortable chairs with nearby power outlets for their convenience.

Your suggestions are important to us, and we will take them all into consideration. We want to make sure that the cafeteria is a place where you can come to enjoy a healthy meal, socialize with your friends, and take a break from your busy day.
