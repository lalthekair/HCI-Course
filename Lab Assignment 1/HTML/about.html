Our Story

Our journey began in 2012.

Our mission is to provide our students, staff, and community with a delicious, nutritious, and sustainable dining experience. We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment where everyone feels comfortable and respected. We are committed to using fresh, high-quality ingredients and to offering a variety of healthy and affordable options. We also believe in supporting local farmers and businesses, and we are committed to reducing our environmental impact.

We are committed to multiple values.
We believe that everyone deserves access to healthy food. We offer a variety of nutritious options, including fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean protein.
We are committed to reducing our environmental impact. We use fresh, local ingredients whenever possible, and we compost our food scraps.
We believe that food is a powerful tool for bringing people together. We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment where everyone feels comfortable and respected.
We believe that everyone should have access to affordable food. We offer a variety of affordable options, and we accept all forms of payment.
